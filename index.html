Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I use the app offline?

A: Yes! Once articles are downloaded, you can read them offline. The app will sync your progress when you reconnect to the internet.

Q: Which CEFR level should I choose?

A: Start with a level that feels comfortable for you. If you're unsure, try A2-A1 first. You can always switch levels qnytime.

Q: The audio/text-to-speech feature isn't working

A: Please check your device's volume settings and ensure the app has audio permissions. Also, make sure your device isn't in silent mode.

Q: How often is new content added?

A: We regularly deliver one for each category, two articles in total. Check the app everyday!.

Q: Can I bookmark articles for later reading?

A: Absolutely! Use the bookmark feature to save articles you want to revisit. You can find all your bookmarked articles in the Bookmarks tab.
