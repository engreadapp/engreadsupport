Readicle Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 11, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Readicle ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application ("App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data responsibly.

2. Information We Collect

Information We DO Collect:

  • Usage Data: Article reading progress, learning preferences (stored locally)
  • Device Information: iOS version, device model (anonymized for app improvement)
  • App Performance: Crash reports, performance metrics (anonymized)

Information We DO NOT Collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Contacts or photos
  • Payment information (handled entirely by Apple)

3. How We Use Information

The limited information we collect is used to:

  • Provide and maintain the App's functionality
  • Track your personal learning progress (stored locally on your device)
  • Improve the App's performance and user experience
  • Fix bugs and resolve technical issues

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, rent, or share your personal information with third parties.

Your learning progress and preferences are stored locally on your device and are not transmitted to our servers.

5. Apple App Store and Subscriptions

All subscription purchases are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not store or have access to your payment information. Apple handles all billing, payment processing, and customer service for subscriptions according to their privacy policy.

6. Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: Your reading progress and app preferences are stored locally on your device
  • No Cloud Storage: We do not store personal data on external servers
  • Anonymous Analytics: Only anonymous, aggregated usage statistics are collected
  • Security: All data transmission uses industry-standard encryption

7. Children's Privacy

Our App is designed for general educational use and is not specifically directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

8. Analytics and Performance

We may use anonymized analytics to understand how users interact with our App. This helps us improve functionality and user experience. All analytics data is aggregated and cannot be used to identify individual users.

9. Your Rights and Choices

  • Data Access: Your learning data is stored locally and accessible only to you
  • Data Deletion: Uninstalling the App removes all locally stored data
  • Subscription Management: Manage subscriptions through iOS Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions
  • Opt-out: You can disable analytics in iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Analytics & Improvements

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will post the updated policy in the App and update the "Effective Date." Your continued use of the App after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Third-Party Services

Our App does not integrate with third-party social media or advertising networks. All core functionality is provided directly by us or Apple's services.

12. International Users

If you are using our App from outside the United States, please note that your information may be transferred to and processed in the United States, where our servers are located.
